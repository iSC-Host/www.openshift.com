Training & Certification

OpenShift Enterprise Administration (CL280)

System Administrators will learn how to deploy, configure, and maintain OpenShift Enterprise broker and OpenShift Enterprise node hosts. Through hands-on labs, they will also learn how to deploy and manage applications in the cloud. Available as:

  • Virtual Classroom (CL280VT) [North America & Europe]
  • Online Learning (CL280R) [North America, Europe, & Asia-Pacific]
  • On-site (private team training)
Red Hat Certificate of Expertise in Platform-as-a-Service (EX280)

An IT professional who has earned the Certificate of Expertise in Platform-as-a-Service has demonstrated the skills, knowledge, and abilities needed to create, configure, and manage a cloud application platform using OpenShift Enterprise by Red Hat©.

  • Red Hat Certificate of Expertise in PaaS Exam (EX280)
OpenShift Enterprise Application Development (CL275)

Developers and devops professionals will learn how to create applications in Openshift Enterprise by Red Hat, expand OpenShift Enterprise’s features creating cartridges, as well as how to identify performance problems and troubleshoot with tuning techniques. Available as:

  • Classroom (CL275)
  • Virtual Classroom (CL275VT) [North America & Europe]
  • Online Learning (CL275R) [North America, Europe, & Asia-Pacific]
  • On-site (private team training)
